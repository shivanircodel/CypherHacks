Refugee Help

Refugee Aid is a project that investigates the present conditions and issues facing Ukrainians. Refugee Help is a website that tries to assist refugees by allowing them to find a place to stay, share their articles, and connect with those who are eager to help. We also share statistics on many urgent concerns of refugees so that they can gain a better understanding of what is going on around them.

Learn more

Features

Refugee Help offers useful elements that can make their lives easier, even if only in a small way.
The following features are:

Lend A Room.

We've designed a system that allows people to publish the availability of rooms, as well as the numbers of people who can stay, so that refugees can find a place to stay during these hard times.

Blogs

A blog where people may post their views and read others' to learn about their conditions and difficulties.

Stats

A statistic to visualise a problem and make it understandable to everybody, especially those who aren't familiar with technology.

Inspiration

We were inspired to create Refugee Help after witnessing the current scenario and hardships that Ukrainians are going through.
